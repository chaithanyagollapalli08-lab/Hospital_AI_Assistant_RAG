--- CASHLESS FACILITY ---

What is Cashless?
  - You do not pay upfront from your pocket; your insurer settles the bill directly with us.
  - Available for IN-PATIENT (IPD) admissions only.
  - NOT available for OPD consultations or diagnostic tests (except select plans).

How to Avail Cashless:
  Step 1: Inform us at the time of admission that you wish to use insurance
  Step 2: Submit your Insurance Policy Card / Health Card + Photo ID (Aadhar/PAN)
  Step 3: Our Insurance Desk will contact your TPA for pre-authorization
  Step 4: TPA will approve or send a query (usually within 4-6 hours)
  Step 5: After approval, you are admitted without paying for covered expenses
  Step 6: At discharge, sign the final bill. Non-covered amounts must be paid by you.

Insurance Desk Location: Ground Floor, Near Main Reception
Insurance Desk Hours: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM (Mon–Sat) | 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM (Sunday)
Insurance Desk Phone: +91-40-12345699
Email: insurance@cityhospital.in

--- REIMBURSEMENT CLAIMS ---

If Cashless Was Not Possible:
  - You pay the bill first, then claim from your insurer.
  - We provide all required documents for reimbursement.

Documents We Provide for Reimbursement:
  - Original Discharge Summary
  - Original Final Bill with itemization
  - Doctor's Certificate / Medical Certificate
  - All Investigation Reports (lab, radiology)
  - Operation Notes (if surgery was done)
  - Pharmacy bills with prescriptions
  - Indoor Case Papers (on request from insurance company)

How to Request Reimbursement Documents:
  - Visit Medical Records Department, 1st Floor
  - Bring your claim form from insurance company
  - Documents ready within 3–5 working days
  - Soft copies available on email request

--- WHAT IS TYPICALLY COVERED ---

Usually Covered:
  ✅ Hospitalization (Room, Nursing, ICU)
  ✅ Surgery costs
  ✅ Medicines during hospitalization
  ✅ Pre-hospitalization investigations (30–60 days prior – plan-dependent)
  ✅ Post-hospitalization follow-up (60–90 days – plan-dependent)
  ✅ Daycare procedures (Dialysis, Cataract, Chemotherapy, etc.)

Usually NOT Covered (General Exclusions):
  ❌ OPD consultations (most basic plans)
  ❌ Cosmetic procedures
  ❌ Dental (unless due to accident)
  ❌ Pre-existing diseases (during waiting period)
  ❌ Infertility treatments (some plans exclude)
  ❌ Non-prescription consumables
  ❌ Alcohol-related ailments

Note: Coverage varies significantly by policy. Always verify with your insurer.

--- FREQUENTLY ASKED INSURANCE QUESTIONS ---

Q: Do you accept Star Health Insurance?
A: Yes, we accept Star Health & Allied Insurance for cashless and reimbursement claims.

Q: Can I use insurance for an OPD visit?
A: Generally, OPD is not covered under cashless. Some premium plans include OPD coverage. Please check your policy documents or speak with our insurance desk.

Q: How long does cashless approval take?
A: Pre-authorization typically takes 4–8 hours. Planned admissions should apply 24–48 hours in advance.

Q: What if my cashless is rejected?
A: You can proceed with reimbursement. Pay the bill and we'll provide all documents needed for your claim.

Q: Is there a room rent sub-limit I should know about?
A: Yes, many policies have room rent limits (e.g., 1% of Sum Insured per day). Choosing a room above your eligible category may result in proportionate deduction. Our billing team will guide you.
