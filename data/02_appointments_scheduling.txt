--- BOOKING CHANNELS ---

1. ONLINE BOOKING (Recommended)
   Website: www.cityhospital.in/appointments
   App: City Hospital App (Available on Android & iOS)
   Steps:
     Step 1: Open website or app
     Step 2: Click on "Book Appointment"
     Step 3: Select Department / Specialization
     Step 4: Choose your preferred Doctor
     Step 5: Select available Date and Time Slot
     Step 6: Enter Patient Details (Name, Age, Mobile Number)
     Step 7: Pay consultation fee online (optional, can pay at counter)
     Step 8: Receive confirmation SMS and WhatsApp message with appointment ID

2. PHONE BOOKING
   Call: +91-40-12345678 / +91-40-87654321
   WhatsApp: +91-9876543210
   Timing: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM (Monday to Saturday)
              9:00 AM – 2:00 PM (Sunday)
   Steps: Provide Name, Age, Department, Preferred Date, and Doctor Name

3. WALK-IN (Counter Booking)
   Location: Main Reception, Ground Floor
   Timing: 7:30 AM – 7:00 PM (Mon–Sat) | 8:00 AM – 2:00 PM (Sunday)
   Token system: Walk-in tokens issued on first-come first-served basis
   Note: Walk-in slots are limited; online booking is always preferred.

4. WHATSAPP BOOKING
   Message "BOOK" to +91-9876543210
   Bot will guide you step by step

--- OPD TIMINGS ---

General OPD (All departments): 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM
Morning Session: 8:00 AM – 1:00 PM
Evening Session: 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM
Closed: Sundays (Emergency open 24x7)

--- SLOT AVAILABILITY RULES ---

- Slots open 7 days in advance
- Maximum 2 appointments per patient per week for same department
- Slot duration per patient: 15 minutes (General) | 30 minutes (Post-operative)
- Online slots: 70% of total capacity
- Walk-in slots: 30% of total capacity reserved
- Cancellation: Must be done 2 hours before appointment time

--- RESCHEDULING & CANCELLATION ---

How to Reschedule:
  - Online: Login → My Appointments → Reschedule
  - Phone: Call reception with Appointment ID
  - Rescheduling allowed up to 2 times per booking

How to Cancel:
  - Cancel via App, Website, or Phone
  - Cancellation fee: None if cancelled 2+ hours before appointment
  - Late cancellation fee: ₹50

Refund Policy:
  - Online payment cancellation: Refunded within 5-7 working days to original payment method
  - Counter payment: Refunded same day at the billing counter

--- APPOINTMENT REMINDERS ---

- SMS reminder: 24 hours before appointment
- WhatsApp reminder: 2 hours before appointment
- Patients must arrive 15 minutes before scheduled time
- Bring: Previous prescriptions, reports, Aadhar card (or hospital ID card)

--- SPECIAL APPOINTMENT TYPES ---

1. Emergency Appointment
   Available 24x7
   Walk directly to Emergency Department
   No prior booking required

2. Follow-up Appointment
   Valid within 30 days of first visit (same doctor)
   Reduced fee: ₹100 for follow-up (instead of full consultation fee)
   Mention "Follow-up" while booking

3. Video Consultation (Telemedicine)
   Available for selected doctors
   Platform: City Hospital App / Zoom link sent via SMS
   Fee: ₹300 flat for video consultation
   Available: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Mon–Sat)

4. Second Opinion Consultation
   Available for Oncology, Cardiology, Neurology
   Requires upload of previous reports
   Fee: ₹1,000
